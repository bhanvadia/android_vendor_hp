Welcome to Classicnerd


Classicnerd [Klas-ik-nurd] (noun Slang)

"A multi faceted team obsessed with an enthusiastic hobby or pursuit of the highest quality".

First and foremost we would like to give credit where credit is due. We admire, respect, and appreciate all the talents and modding that CyanogenMOD, Evervolv, and Team AOKP have shown to help bring new life to the HP Touchpad. They are truly the professionals here and we all owe a great bit of gratitude to bringing such pleasure to our device. Also, Android (AOSP) for giving us an open source environment to which we can expand and bring new ideas to life. We also would like to thank all of the team members and supporters who helped bring this team from a dream to a reality. It was because of you guys/gals that we have assembled a truly fun team open with ideas and possibilities. Now for the introduction of Classicnerd.
